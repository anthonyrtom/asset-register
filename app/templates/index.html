This app calculates depreciation over the useful life of an asset

  1. Depreciation Method is either straight line or reducing balance, no ther method allowed

  3. Please check your file that it has been done correctly, you use the app at your own risk
  4. If your file is in the correct format, it will bedowloaded automatically after depreciation has been calculated
